Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SowSuccess

Is this a donation or investment?

This is neither a donation nor an investment. It's a contribution towards premium training and community building. You receive high-value training worth ‚Çπ3999 and the opportunity to earn through referrals.

Do I need special skills to refer others?

No special skills required! We provide comprehensive training on how to share this opportunity effectively. Your genuine enthusiasm and the program's value will speak for itself.

Can I do both training programs?

Currently, each registration gives access to one training program. However, you can join again for the second program with another contribution to access both training tracks.

How does the 4x9 network model work?

You refer 4 people, each of them refers 4, and so on for 9 levels. You earn from each level, creating exponential income potential as your network grows.
